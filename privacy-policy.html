Introduction

At Focusphere, we are committed to your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data and privacy for the Focusphere application ("App") available on iOS, macOS, and watchOS platforms.

Our Privacy Commitment

Focusphere is designed with complete privacy and data autonomy as our foundation:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect any user data.
  • No Internet Access: Our app functions completely offline and does not connect to the internet.
  • 100% Local Processing: All data generated by your usage stays exclusively on your device.

Information Storage

All information generated through your use of Focusphere is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Fully under your control
  • Accessible only to you

Local Data Types

While we don't collect any data, the app does store the following information locally on your device:

  • Focus Session Data: Duration, completion status, and timestamps of your focus sessions
  • App Preferences: Your customized settings for timers, block lists, and app appearance
  • App Usage Statistics: If you enable the Guard Mode feature, information about which apps you're attempting to use during focus sessions

Cross-Device Functionality

For synchronization between your Apple devices, Focusphere uses Apple's built-in iCloud and CloudKit framework. This means:

  • Synchronization happens directly between your devices using your personal iCloud account
  • No data passes through our servers
  • All synchronization adheres to Apple's privacy standards and encryption

Your Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can view all your session data within the App
  • You can delete your data through the App settings at any time
  • Uninstalling the App will remove all associated data from your device

Third-Party Access

Since we don't collect your data and the app doesn't connect to the internet, we do not and cannot share your information with any third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
