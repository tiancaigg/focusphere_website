Introduction

At Focusphere, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Focusphere application ("App") available on iOS and macOS platforms.

Information We Collect

Focusphere is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary for the functioning of the app:

  • Focus Session Data: We collect data about your focus sessions, including duration, completion status, and timestamps. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: For our device synchronization feature, we collect basic device identifiers to facilitate connections between your devices. This information is only used for establishing direct peer-to-peer connections.
  • App Usage Data: If you enable the Guard Mode feature, the app will need permission to monitor which apps you're attempting to use during focus sessions. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our App's functionality
  • To enable cross-device synchronization of your focus sessions
  • To generate statistics and insights about your focus habits (displayed only to you)
  • To improve our App based on aggregate, anonymized usage patterns

Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is important to us:

  • All focus session data is stored locally on your devices.
  • When synchronizing between devices, data is transmitted directly between your devices using Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework without passing through our servers.
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregate data for analytical purposes, but this will never include personally identifiable information.

Your Rights

You have control over your data:

  • You can view all your session data within the App.
  • You can delete your data through the App settings.
  • You can opt out of device synchronization at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us
